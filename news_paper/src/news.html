6 killed, three injured after car falls into well in Jharkhand's Hazaribag

By Press Trust of India: Six people were killed and three injured when the SUV in which they were travelling fell into a well in Jharkhand's Hazaribagh district on Tuesday afternoon, an official said. The accident took place near Romi village under Padma police station in Hazaribagh district when the SUV lost control and fell into the roadside well, Hazaribagh SP Manoj Ratan Chouthe told PTI.
